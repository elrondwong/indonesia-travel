每日导游/司机沟通 Checklist（中英文话术）

独立用途：这份文档专门放“每天出发前和现场要跟司机、向导、船家确认什么”。中文用于自己核对，英文可直接发给对方。网页版本可直接打勾，勾选状态保存在当前浏览器本地。

总原则：

  1. 安全优先；天气、路况、火山限制、海况不合适时，按司机/向导/船家判断改备选。
  2. 每天出发前确认：接人时间、目的地 pin、返回硬截止、票/船/向导、天气/海况风险。
  3. 用 Could you please...、If it is safe and allowed...、Please let us know... 这类表达，不用命令式语气。
  4. Bromo、Ijen、Penida 都不要把高风险项目说成“必须完成”；要说“如果安全且允许”。

通用礼貌话术

场景 中文 English
安全优先 今天安全第一。如果天气、路况或规定不适合，我们可以改备选。 Safety comes first today. If the weather, road conditions, or local rules are not suitable, we can switch to the backup plan.
请发定位 麻烦发今天第一个目的地的准确 Google Maps pin。 Could you please send the exact Google Maps pin for our first destination today?
请提前提醒 如果某个点不安全、关闭或太赶，请提前告诉我们。 Please let us know early if any place is unsafe, closed, or too rushed.
摄影需求 我们会拍风景照，可能需要提前到机位和使用三脚架。 We will shoot landscape photos, so we may need to arrive early and use tripods.
不懂时用翻译 如果英文不清楚，我们可以用 WhatsApp 或 Google Translate 确认。 If English is not clear, we can confirm by WhatsApp text or Google Translate.
硬截止 今天有硬截止时间，请帮我们按时返回或出发。 We have a hard deadline today. Please help us leave or return on time.

D6｜6 月 18 日｜Kuta 到 Nusa Penida｜上午浮潜 + 西线快拍 + Kelingking 日落

跟司机/船司/船家确认 Checklist

给司机/船家/船司的话术

用途 中文 English
Sanur 出发 我们 08:30 船去 Nusa Penida，想 06:45-07:00 从 Kuta 出发去 Sanur。 Our boat to Nusa Penida is around 08:30. We would like to leave Kuta for Sanur at 06:45-07:00.
到达港口 请确认船到 Banjar Nyuh、Toya Pakeh 还是其他港口。 Could you please confirm whether the boat arrives at Banjar Nyuh, Toya Pakeh, or another harbor?
接人 pin 请把到达港和司机接人位置的准确 pin 发给我们。 Please send us the exact pin for the arrival harbor and the driver pickup point.
上午浮潜 我们想上午先浮潜，利用比较稳定的海况；请 14:00 前结束浮潜并回到陆地路线。 We would like to snorkel in the morning for better sea conditions. Please finish snorkeling and return to the land route before 14:00.
下午西线顺序 下午请按 Angel's Billabong、Broken Beach、Kelingking Beach、Paluang Cliff 白天景色/日落踩点、Banah Cliff Point、回 Kelingking Beach 日落的顺序安排。 In the afternoon, please arrange the route in this order: Angel's Billabong, Broken Beach, Kelingking Beach, Paluang Cliff for daylight photos and sunset scouting, Banah Cliff Point, then back to Kelingking Beach for sunset.
Kelingking 日落 我们想把 Kelingking 留到 17:30-18:10 拍日落，不要上午和傍晚重复跑两次。 We would like to keep Kelingking for sunset around 17:30-18:10, instead of visiting it twice in the morning and evening.
浮潜安全 浮潜点请按当天海况安排，Manta / Crystal / Gamat / Wall / SD / Mangrove 都只是参考，不需要强行指定。 Please choose snorkeling spots according to today’s sea conditions. Manta, Crystal, Gamat, Wall, SD, and Mangrove are only references, not fixed requirements.
海况调整 如果上午海况不好，请改点、缩短或取消危险点；不要为了 Manta 硬冲。 If the morning sea condition is not good, please change spots, shorten the snorkeling, or cancel risky spots. Please do not force Manta.
Kelingking 下切 Kelingking 可以下到上段安全台阶拍沙滩视角；如果完整下到底部沙滩，我们会取消 Paluang、Banah 和回拍日落。 For Kelingking, we may go down to the upper safe steps for the beach view. If we hike all the way down to the beach, we will cancel Paluang, Banah, and the sunset return.
Paluang Paluang Cliff 和 Kelingking 很近，请下午先带我们拍白天景色并踩日落点；如果 Kelingking 人太多或光位更好，也可作为日落候选。 Paluang Cliff is close to Kelingking. Please take us there in the afternoon for daylight photos and sunset scouting. If Kelingking is too crowded or Paluang has better light, it can also be a sunset option.
Banah Banah Cliff Point 只有在路干、安全、并且 17:30 前能回 Kelingking 时才去。 We will go to Banah Cliff Point only if the road is dry and safe, and only if we can return to Kelingking before 17:30.

当日常见场景沟通

场景 中文 English
海况差 如果海况不好，请取消危险点，安全第一。 If the sea condition is not good, please cancel the risky spots. Safety first.
浮潜点变化 潜点临时变化没关系，请按浪、流和能见度选择安全点。 It is okay if the snorkeling spots change. Please choose safe spots based on waves, current, and visibility.
浮潜拖延 如果浮潜拖延，请直接压缩 Angel's Billabong / Broken Beach 或 Banah，不要压缩 Kelingking 日落。 If snorkeling runs late, please shorten Angel's Billabong / Broken Beach or Banah, not the Kelingking sunset window.
上午必须去 Kelingking 如果路线必须上午经过 Kelingking，请只停 5-10 分钟确认机位，傍晚再正式拍。 If the route must pass Kelingking in the morning, please stop only 5-10 minutes for scouting, and we will shoot properly at sunset.
Banah 来不及 如果 17:30 前回不到 Kelingking，请直接取消 Banah。 If we cannot return to Kelingking before 17:30, please cancel Banah directly.
日落后返程 日落后请直接送我们回 Coco Resort，不加野路或额外悬崖点。 After sunset, please take us directly back to Coco Resort. No extra cliff stops or rough roads.
